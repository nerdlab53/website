i'll be graduating soon

posted on : 7th jan 2025 | reading time : 2 minutes

it feels like just yesterday when i was wandering in my college as a freshman, sometimes building stuff in the robotics lab, chilling with my seniors, organizing robotics competitions etc.

my college wasn't that great, i mean it's a tier 3 college but i have loved every bit of this life, and I will always be grateful for it as it did provide me with good enough opportunities.

it's been one heck of a ride and i'd agree to do it all again in a split second. i've got one last chance to organize the robotics competitions this year, let's see how it goes.

one. last. time.
